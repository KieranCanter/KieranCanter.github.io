Question 9¶

There appears to be strong correlations between model residuals and continent. For example, Europe and Oceania have significantly larger and more concentrated distributions while Africa has a long, stretched distribution that is centered much lower. This suggests that the regression analysis conducted on the gross data cannot be as accurate and reliable as separate regression analyses done on the data by continent.
